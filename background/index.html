I grew up in Strafford, Vermont; a tiny New England town with about a thousand residents. When people talk of small towns, they often refer to them as “single stop-light towns,” well, we’re a single stop-light county. After attending a small private school in the neightboring town, I first attended a small liberal art college before transferring 2000 miles away to Baylor University in Waco, Texas.

After moving around the South for a few years, I moved back to my New England roots to obtain a bachelor’s in business administration. A few weeks after graduating I discovered coding and things took off from there. I simply fell in love with tech: launching my first website a week into coding was something I originally believed to be impossible.

I cannot wait to serve you through code. I have always been incredibly people-oriented and have been a helping hand all my life. Combining the desire to help with the tech world has opened new doors for me, and truly ignited something in me I didn’t know was there. I am confident I can help you meet all of your front-end development needs and more!

When I’m not coding, you can find me cruising in my jeep, cuddling my beagle, or doing anything outdoorsy (running, hiking, paddle boarding, etc.). I look forward to getting to know you, too!

-Kay
